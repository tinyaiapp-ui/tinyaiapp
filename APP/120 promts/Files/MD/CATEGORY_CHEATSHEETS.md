# Category Cheatsheets

Use this as a fast prep guide before running prompts.

## 1) CV / Resume
Prepare:
- target role title
- target industry
- current CV text
- target job description
- 3-5 measurable achievements

Good output goals:
- 1 page
- strong action verbs
- ATS keywords included naturally

## 2) LinkedIn
Prepare:
- current headline/about
- top 5 skills
- target role
- short career summary

Good output goals:
- searchable headline
- clear About section (Hook / Proof / CTA)
- concise outreach messages

## 3) Cover Letter
Prepare:
- target company name
- role title
- company mission/values snippet
- your 2-3 strongest achievements

Good output goals:
- specific and tailored
- no clichés
- one clear closing CTA

## 4) Interview
Prepare:
- job description
- 4 STAR stories (challenge, action, result)
- one weakness + improvement plan
- salary expectations range

Good output goals:
- answers under 90 sec
- quantifiable outcomes
- natural, non-robotic tone

## 5) Job Search & Outreach
Prepare:
- target companies list
- job links and status
- outreach targets (recruiter/hiring manager/referral)
- follow-up schedule

Good output goals:
- reusable message templates
- weekly cadence
- clear tracker status

## 6) Negotiation & Follow-up
Prepare:
- offer amount and benefits
- market range (city/country)
- personal priorities (salary, remote, PTO, growth)
- deadline for response

Good output goals:
- polite + firm tone
- options-based negotiation
- documented decision rubric
