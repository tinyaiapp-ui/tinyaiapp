# JobBoost Prompt Kit — 120 Prompts

## Quick cheat sheet (how to use)
1. Copy one prompt.
2. Replace every **[bold bracket field]** with your data.
3. Paste into any AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.).
4. If output is weak, add: “Use stronger metrics and ATS keywords from the job ad.”
5. Ask for 2-3 variants and keep the best one.
