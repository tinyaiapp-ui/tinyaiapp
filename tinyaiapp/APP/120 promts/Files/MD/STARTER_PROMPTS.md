# JobBoost Prompt Kit — Starter (60 Prompts)

## Quick cheat sheet (how to use)
1. Copy one prompt.
2. Replace every **[bold bracket field]** with your data.
3. Paste into any AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.).
4. Ask for “short, ATS-friendly, specific wording.”
5. Generate 2 versions and choose the better one.
